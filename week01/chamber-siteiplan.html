Site Plan Document

Site Name

The site name is Botanical Gardens Chamber. This name represents a city in which the chamber operates and includes the word "Chamber" to clearly indicate the site's purpose.

Site Purpose

Botanical Gardens Chamber of Commerce website aims to:

  • Provide a platform for businesses to connect and network
  • Offer resources and information for economic and community development

Scenarios

Target audience scenarios:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

Color palette:

  • Primary color: Navy blue (#032B44) for headings and accents
  • Secondary color: Light gray (#F7F7F7) for background and paragraphs

Typography

Font selections:

  • Heading font: Open Sans for headings and titles
  • Body font: Lato for paragraphs and body text
